My Web Development and UI/UX Journey

October 2,2021 by Imani Clarke

Before I discovered Web Development and UI/UX I was a clueless 1st year computing student lost in the sea of the many I.T careers out there. Combating that along with my general inexperience with coding(a skill every computing student should have *cries in imposter syndrome*)I was like a fish out of water.At first I had a very cliche view of what it looked like working in Tech. I pictured the stereotypical dude with glasses and all the theatrics, I wasn't like that, at all.So I had to do some thinking and research and ask myself "What do you actually want to do Imani?"

I knew I didn't want to do any of that machine learning stuff *barf* nor the Math *double barf*, but alas I knew what I wanted to be apart of. I wanted to create things that would be useful for someone, I cared alot about what a user would experience with a product and I liked the fact that I would be able to be apart of a team that could create something that could directly affect a user. Hence, my venture into Web Development and UI/UX was born :D. And as a complete beginner it's a long journey ahead of learning but I can't wait to experience it.
